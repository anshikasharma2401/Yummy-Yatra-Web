Description:

Aloo Tikki is a crispy North Indian street food made from mashed potatoes, spices, and herbs. It's shallow-fried until golden and served hot with chutneys.

Prep Time:

‚è±Ô∏è 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 3 medium potatoes (boiled & mashed)
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp dry mango powder (amchur)
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander
  • 2 tbsp cornflour or breadcrumbs
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for shallow frying

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine mashed potatoes, spices, coriander, salt, and cornflour. Mix well.
  2. Shape into small round tikkis and flatten slightly.
  3. Heat oil in a pan. Place tikkis and shallow-fry on medium heat.
  4. Flip when one side is golden and crispy. Cook the other side similarly.
  5. Serve hot with green chutney or tamarind sauce.
