Baingan Bharta

Description:

Baingan Bharta is a flavorful North Indian dish made by roasting eggplants and cooking them with onions, tomatoes, and spices. It’s smoky, spicy, and pairs perfectly with roti or paratha.

Prep Time:

⏱️ 35 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 large eggplant (baingan)
  • 1 large onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 green chili (chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Roast the eggplant on an open flame until skin is charred and soft. Cool, peel and mash.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add onions and sauté until golden. Add green chili and ginger-garlic paste, sauté briefly.
  4. Add tomatoes, turmeric, red chili, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  5. Add mashed baingan and mix well. Cook for 5-7 minutes.
  6. Sprinkle garam masala and mix again.
  7. Garnish with chopped coriander. Serve hot with roti or paratha.
