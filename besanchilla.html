Description:

Besan Cheela is a savory Indian pancake made with gram flour, onions, and fresh herbs. It's quick to prepare and a healthy option for breakfast or snacks.

Prep Time:

‚è±Ô∏è 15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 green chili (chopped)
  • Chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as required
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix besan, onion, chili, coriander, turmeric, and salt.
  2. Add water gradually to form a smooth, pourable batter.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan and lightly grease with oil.
  4. Pour a ladle of batter and spread it into a circle.
  5. Cook on both sides until golden brown using a little oil.
  6. Serve hot with green chutney or ketchup.
