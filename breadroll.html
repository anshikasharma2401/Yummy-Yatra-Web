Description:

Bread Rolls are crispy and golden snacks filled with a spiced mashed potato stuffing and deep-fried to perfection. They‚Äôre a popular tea-time snack in Indian homes.

Prep Time:

Ingredients:

  • 6 bread slices (edges trimmed)
  • 2 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1 green chili (chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped coriander leaves
  • Water to soak bread
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the filling by mixing mashed potatoes, spices, and coriander.
  2. Dip a bread slice in water, squeeze out excess water gently.
  3. Place the filling in the center, roll and seal edges tightly.
  4. Repeat for all slices.
  5. Heat oil in a kadhai and deep fry rolls until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with green chutney or ketchup.
