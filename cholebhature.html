Description:

Chole Bhature is a classic North Indian dish featuring spicy chickpeas (chole) served with deep-fried bread (bhature). It's a hearty and flavorful meal enjoyed by all age groups.

Prep Time:

‚è±Ô∏è 45 minutes

Ingredients:

For Chole:
  • 1 cup chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 2 onions (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp chole masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
For Bhature:
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 1/2 cup curd (yogurt)
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  1. Pressure cook soaked chickpeas with salt until soft.
  2. Heat oil, add cumin, then saut√© onions till golden. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook.
  3. Add tomato puree and spices. Cook till oil separates.
  4. Add boiled chickpeas. Simmer for 15 minutes. Adjust seasoning.
  5. For Bhature, mix flour, curd, soda, and salt. Knead to a soft dough. Rest for 2 hours.
  6. Divide dough, roll into rounds and deep fry till puffed and golden.
  7. Serve hot chole with fluffy bhature, onions, and pickle.
