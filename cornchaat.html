Description:

Corn Chaat is a tangy and spicy Indian snack made with boiled sweet corn, spices, herbs, and lemon juice. It's quick, healthy, and super delicious!

Prep Time:

‚è±Ô∏è 10 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sweet corn (boiled)
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tomato (finely chopped)
  • 1 green chili (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp chaat masala
  • 1/4 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped coriander leaves

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix boiled sweet corn, onion, tomato, and green chili.
  2. Add chaat masala, red chili powder, salt, and lemon juice.
  3. Mix everything well and garnish with coriander leaves.
  4. Serve immediately for best flavor and crunch.
