Description:

Vegetable Cutlets are crispy patties made with mashed veggies and spices, shallow fried to golden perfection. A perfect evening snack or starter.

Prep Time:

‚è±Ô∏è 25 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 boiled potatoes
  • 1/2 cup chopped mixed vegetables (carrot, beans, peas)
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp breadcrumbs
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped)
  • Chopped coriander leaves
  • Oil for shallow frying

Instructions:

  1. Boil and mash all vegetables. Mix in spices, salt, breadcrumbs, and coriander.
  2. Shape into round cutlets.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and shallow fry both sides until golden brown.
  4. Serve hot with mint chutney or ketchup.
