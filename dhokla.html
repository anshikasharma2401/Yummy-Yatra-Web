Description:

Dhokla is a soft and spongy Gujarati snack made from fermented gram flour batter. It's steamed, light, and served with tempered mustard seeds and green chilies.

Prep Time:

‚è±Ô∏è 25 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1 tbsp semolina (rava)
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp ENO fruit salt
  • Salt to taste
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 2 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander and grated coconut for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix besan, semolina, turmeric, salt, sugar, lemon juice, and water to form a smooth batter.
  2. Add ENO and stir quickly. Pour into greased steaming dish immediately.
  3. Steam for 15‚Äì20 mins until a toothpick comes out clean. Let it cool.
  4. Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds and green chilies. Pour over the dhokla.
  5. Garnish with chopped coriander and coconut if desired.
  6. Cut into pieces and serve with green chutney.
