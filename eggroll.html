Description:

Egg Roll is a popular Indian street food made with a flaky paratha filled with a fried egg, vegetables, and tangy sauces.

Prep Time:

‚è±Ô∏è 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 2 parathas or flatbreads
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 small onion (sliced)
  • 1 small cucumber (julienned)
  • 1 green chili (chopped)
  • Chaat masala to taste
  • Tomato ketchup & green chutney
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or butter for frying

Instructions:

  1. Heat a tawa and cook the paratha until golden brown from both sides.
  2. Break one egg on the tawa, sprinkle some salt and place the paratha on top of it. Press gently so the egg sticks to the paratha.
  3. Flip and cook for a few seconds. Remove and keep warm.
  4. Spread ketchup and chutney, add onions, cucumber, green chili, and sprinkle chaat masala.
  5. Roll it tightly and wrap in foil or butter paper. Serve hot!
