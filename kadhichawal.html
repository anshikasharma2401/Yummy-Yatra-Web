Description:

Kadhi Chawal is a popular North Indian dish made with a yogurt-based gram flour curry and served with steamed rice. It's tangy, comforting, and often includes gram flour fritters (pakoras).

Prep Time:

‚è±Ô∏è 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup yogurt (curd)
  • 2 tbsp besan (gram flour)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Optional: fried pakoras (gram flour fritters)
  • Cooked rice (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, whisk yogurt, besan, water, turmeric, red chili powder, and salt until smooth.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard and cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Pour in the yogurt mixture and stir continuously to avoid curdling.
  4. Simmer the kadhi for 20‚Äì25 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add fried pakoras if desired and cook for 5 more minutes.
  6. Serve hot with steamed rice.
