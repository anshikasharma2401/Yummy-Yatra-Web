Description:

Kathi Rolls are a popular Indian street food made by wrapping spicy fillings in soft parathas. A delicious grab-and-go meal!

Prep Time:

‚è±Ô∏è 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 4 parathas or wraps
  • 1 cup paneer or chicken (cooked and spiced)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 tomato (sliced)
  • 1 green chili (chopped)
  • Green chutney
  • Chaat masala
  • Mayonnaise or sauce (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the filling with your choice of protein and spices.
  2. Warm the parathas on a pan.
  3. Spread green chutney and mayo on each paratha.
  4. Place the filling in the center with onions and tomatoes.
  5. Sprinkle chaat masala and roll them tightly.
  6. Wrap in foil or parchment and serve hot.
