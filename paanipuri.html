Description:

Pani Puri is a beloved Indian street food consisting of crispy puris filled with spicy tangy water, potatoes, chickpeas, and tamarind chutney.

Prep Time:

‚è±Ô∏è 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 20 puris (store-bought or homemade)
  • 2 boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1/2 cup boiled chickpeas
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • Mint-coriander water (pani)
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Black salt, cumin powder, chaat masala to taste

Instructions:

  1. Mix mashed potatoes, chickpeas, onions, and spices for the filling.
  2. Crack open the puris from the top.
  3. Add the filling and drizzle with tamarind chutney.
  4. Pour the chilled mint-coriander pani into each puri.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy the burst of flavors!
