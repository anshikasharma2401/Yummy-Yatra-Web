Description:

Pakoras are deep-fried Indian fritters made with gram flour and a variety of vegetables. They are crispy, flavorful, and perfect for tea-time or rainy days.

Prep Time:

‚è±Ô∏è 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup besan (gram flour)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 potato (thinly sliced)
  • 1/2 cup spinach (chopped)
  • 1 green chili (chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp ajwain (carom seeds)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix besan with chili powder, ajwain, salt, and chopped vegetables.
  2. Add water gradually to form a thick batter.
  3. Heat oil in a deep pan on medium heat.
  4. Drop spoonfuls of batter into the oil and fry until golden and crispy.
  5. Remove and drain excess oil on tissue paper.
  6. Serve hot with mint chutney or ketchup.
