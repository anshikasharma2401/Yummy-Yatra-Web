Description:

Paneer Tikka is a popular Indian appetizer made from marinated paneer cubes grilled or baked to perfection. It's flavorful, smoky, and a vegetarian delight!

Prep Time:

‚è±Ô∏è 30 minutes (plus marination)

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 1/2 cup thick curd (yogurt)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp chaat masala
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion and 1 capsicum (cut into cubes)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix curd with spices, lemon juice, salt, and oil to form a marinade.
  2. Add paneer cubes, onion, and capsicum. Mix well and marinate for 30 minutes.
  3. Thread paneer and veggies alternately on skewers.
  4. Grill in a preheated oven at 200¬∞C or on a hot pan until charred and cooked evenly.
  5. Serve hot with mint chutney and lemon wedges.
