Description:

Samosa is a classic Indian snack featuring a crispy, flaky pastry filled with spicy mashed potatoes and peas. It's perfect with tea or as a party snack.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder

  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying and kneading
  • Water as needed

  4. Divide dough into small balls. Roll each into an oval shape and cut in half.
  5. Form a cone with each half, fill with potato mixture, and seal the edges with water.
  6. Deep fry the samosas on medium heat until golden and crispy.
  7. Serve hot with green chutney or tamarind sauce.
