Description:

Sooji Halwa (Semolina Pudding) is a classic Indian sweet dish made with roasted semolina, ghee, sugar, and flavored with cardamom. It‚Äôs quick, delicious, and commonly made during festivals or pooja.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup sooji (semolina)
  • 1/2 cup ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2.5 cups water
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • Chopped dry fruits (cashews, almonds, raisins)
  • Few saffron strands (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil water in a pan, add sugar, saffron (optional), and keep it hot.
  2. In a separate pan, heat ghee and roast the sooji on medium heat until golden and aromatic.
  3. Carefully add the hot sugar water to the roasted sooji, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  4. Add cardamom powder and cook until mixture thickens and ghee starts separating.
  5. Garnish with fried cashews, almonds, and raisins. Serve hot.
